The Rabbit - 兔 - tù

The rabbit is the fourth zodiac in the 12-year Chinese Zodiac cycle. You are a rabbit if you are born in 1915, 1927, 1939, 1951, 1963, 1975, 1987, 1999, 2011, 2023...


The rabbit is regarded as a tame creature symbolizing hope.They tend to be docile and silent. When they are lieing down, they may look lazy and weak but tonce on the move, they re fast and agile. People born in the Year of the Rabbit tend to be approachable and non-agressive. They are noble and well-mannered.The rabbit is most compatible with others born in the year of the rabbits, pig, and goat.
